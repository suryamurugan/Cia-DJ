About

ATRIA'S BIGGEST TINKERING PLAYGROUND

30 hours of Fun Engineering

CIA Hackathon aims at bringing together engineers, designers, and students to hack and build life-changing solutions using rapid prototyping techniques. CIA Hackathon is a 30-hour event which will be held at Atria Institute of Technology on the 14 and 15 of March, 2020. The vision of CIA Hackathon is to identify and solve problems in improving the lives of the common man and harness technology to creatively demonstrate ideas.
